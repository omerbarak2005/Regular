About Me

Three countries.
One relentless drive.

I was born in 🇮🇱 Israel, raised in 🇲🇽 Mexico, and now competing on the mat and in the boardroom in 🇺🇸 the United States as a Division I wrestler at the University of Pennsylvania.

Growing up across three cultures gave me something most people can't learn in a classroom: the ability to connect with anyone, anywhere. I'm fluent in English, Hebrew, and Spanish — and I carry the mindset of an athlete into every room I walk into.

At Penn, I'm majoring in Biology with minors in Healthcare Management and Consumer Psychology through Wharton. I'm driven by the intersection of healthcare, technology, and capital — where the biggest problems meet the biggest opportunities.

My network spans Bessemer Venture Partners, Goldman Sachs, First Round Capital, Jefferies, BlackRock, Morgan Stanley, and beyond. Every relationship is built on authenticity, relentlessness, and a genuine desire to create value.
